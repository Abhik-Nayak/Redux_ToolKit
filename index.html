About TNC

The Nutrition Circle (TNC) is aimed at people who want to transform themselves into healthy human beings by following the right food habits, always staying fit, and taking care of their own health. TNC believes Food, Body, and Health go hand in hand with each other, and are the most important factors in helping you lead a good life.

An estimate of 108 million Americans are on a diet but a staggering 98% of them gain the lost weight within just one year*! This is because most of the diets given today are so strict and expensive that when people stop following it, they start gaining weight again. At TNC, we want people to love what they eat and hence, we give diets according to what you like eating and what falls in your budget. We want to make this diet easy for you to live with so that you can never go back to unfit again! And let's not forget that along with eating the right food, we will show you the right exercises so that you get back in shape. For those with any health diseases like diabetes, PCOD, hypertension, or high cholesterol amongst many others, we will carefully plan a diet that will help towards curing your health problems. At TNC, we want to give you results that last and a diet that becomes your lifestyle!

* "WDEP Video 1." The Institute for the Psychology of Eating. N.p., n.d. Web. 6 Feb. 2017.
